\entry{Bradley--Terry Analysis of the English Premier League}{\href{https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1mncW87F79ezDnqah3qGSjqWAFXMQzGMNqfB2MTYl6jM/edit}{[Slides]} \,\href{https://github.com/cedricshan/Bradley-Terry-Model-Analysis-of-the-English-Premier-League}{[GitHub]}}{Mar 2026}{}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Fit a \textbf{Bradley--Terry} paired-comparison model --- reformulated as logistic regression for MLE inference --- to \textbf{7{,}860} English Premier League matches across 21 seasons (2005/06--2025/26) and 44 clubs.
  \item Extended the base model with the \textbf{Davidson} formulation for draws (24\% of outcomes) plus season-specific and team-specific home-advantage intercepts, validated via Wald and likelihood-ratio tests against nested baselines.
  \item Estimated home-team odds boost at $\hat{\theta}\!\approx\!1.64\times$ (95\% CI $[1.54, 1.74]$); detected a significant secular decline in home advantage ($r\!=\!-0.64$) and a near-complete collapse during the 2020/21 COVID season ($\hat{\alpha}\!\approx\!-0.10$), implicating spectators causally.
\end{itemize}
